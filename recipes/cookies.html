Cookies

Description

These cookies are exactly the kinds that Joe ate shortly before passing away from food poisoning. (god bless that man) The exact food that killed Joe is unknown, but these cookies sure are to die for! This recipe was passed down to me by my witch mother in law shortly before my divorce. If you want to try these cookies out for yourself, make sure you have all the ingredients listed and if you don't, feel free to substitute with your own ideas!

Ingredients

  • Tongue of Toad
  • Sugar (or "Stevia" for sugar free)
  • Oat Flour
  • Kumanan's Hair
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Allow butter to reach room temperature
  2. Stir butter with sugar (or sugar substitute)
  3. Add in Kumanan's hair
  4. Stir in Oat flour
  5. Mix for 10 minutes on low speed
  6. Snack on some tongue of toad
  7. Bake at 350 degrees celsius for 1 hour
