Brownies

Description

Today I will be showing you guys how to make my World Famous "Beautiful Brownies". I developed this recipe back in my college days when my only source of income was taking money from those people on the street with a bunch of money in their hat (I even got up to $15 once!). These brownies are great if you're on a budget and even better if you're able to purchase some higher-quality ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Nail Clippings
  • Flour
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Sugar
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Allow butter to reach room temperature
  2. Stir butter with cocoa powder
  3. Add in nail clippings
  4. Stir in flour and sugar
  5. Mix for 10 minutes on low speed
  6. Bake at 350 degrees celsius for 10 seconds
